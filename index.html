About city

Innsbruck is one of the best ski resorts in Austria.

The city itself is located in the Inn Valley, at the very foot of the Eastern Alps. Therefore, it is almost impossible to find a place in this city where the mountains are not visible.

Innsbruck in numbers

  • Population: just over 130,000 people
  • Time zone: UTC + 1 in winter and UTC + 2 in summer
  • Ski areas: 9, all connected by transport links
  • Vertical drop: 860 meters - 3100 meters
  • Season duration: from 12 months (December) to 4 months (April) inclusive
